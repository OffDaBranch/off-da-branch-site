Privacy policy

TrapCotton privacy policy

This page explains how TrapCotton handles information submitted through the waitlist and contact forms on this site.

What we collect

We may collect the information you submit directly, including your name, email address, company name, phone number, city interest, and message content.

How we use it

  • Respond to your request
  • Manage waitlist and launch interest
  • Review wholesale, partnership, or licensing inquiries
  • Operate and improve the TrapCotton inquiry workflow

Data handling

Submitted inquiries may be stored in BranchOps-controlled systems and may also be mirrored to operational tools used for follow-up and workflow management.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your submitted information, contact admin@branchoffholdings.com.
