## Competency Rule
Codex may not route work to a role by title alone. Before work begins, Codex must identify the role card, department mission, scope, required knowledge, required skills, allowed work, blocked work, inputs, outputs, validation checklist, reviewer, escalation triggers, source-of-truth updates, and ERB gate.
