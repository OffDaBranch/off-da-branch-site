## Core Rule
Cannabis, hemp, CBD, THC, cannabinoid, dispensary, cultivation, manufacturing, delivery, wholesale, testing, seed-to-sale, packaging, labeling, medical/adult-use, and regulated-product work is regulated-market work.

## Required Intake
1. Jurisdiction
2. Product category
3. Regulated activity
4. License class or requirement
5. Plant-touching or non-plant-touching classification
6. Compliance reviewer
7. Legal/regulatory gate
8. Source-of-truth records
9. Seed-to-sale/batch impact
10. Testing/COA impact
11. Packaging/labeling impact
12. Tax/banking/280E review
13. Security/access review
14. Marketing/public-claims review
15. Founder approval gate
16. Blocked actions
17. Validation evidence

## Allowed Outputs
Compliance checklists, readiness matrices, SOP outlines, source-of-truth schemas, legal/compliance handoff packages, non-final business planning documents, compliance-support software system designs, high-level workflow maps, and risk classifications.

## Blocked Outputs
- No illegal cannabis production instructions.
- No cultivation recipes.
- No extraction parameters.
- No illicit manufacturing instructions.
- No trafficking, smuggling, unlicensed shipping, or evasion tactics.
- No instructions for unlicensed cannabis sales, manufacturing, delivery, or distribution.
- No dosage instructions.
- No medical advice or disease-treatment claims.
- No pregnancy, breastfeeding, pet-health, or unreviewed public claims.
- No license applications, grants, bids, filings, or submissions without founder/legal/compliance approval.
- No recommendations to evade state, federal, municipal, banking, tax, age-gating, advertising, or seed-to-sale requirements.
