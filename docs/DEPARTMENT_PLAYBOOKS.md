## Playbook Rule
Every department playbook includes mission, scope, jobs/roles, knowledge, skills, task types, allowed systems/files, blocked systems/files, default workflow, validation, reviewer chain, escalation, source-of-truth duties, and handoff format. The complete role list for each department is in ROLE_COMPETENCY_MATRIX.md.

## Universal Department Workflow
1. Intake and classify task type.
2. Select department and role cards; role title alone is insufficient.
3. Inspect knowledge map and repo-specific AGENTS.md.
4. Confirm allowed and blocked systems/files.
5. Gather required inputs and identify missing facts.
6. Produce output within scope.
7. Validate using VALIDATION_RUBRICS.md.
8. Route to required reviewer and risk owner when relevant.
9. Identify source-of-truth updates.
10. Submit ERB decision and handoff.

## Universal Handoff Format
Use HANDOFF_TEMPLATE.md for every department handoff.
